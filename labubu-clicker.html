About Labubu Clicker

Labubu Clicker: Dive into cute chaos with this idle clicker featuring the quirky Labubu! Tap for coins, unlock upgrades, tear blind boxes, and build an epic collection of adorable characters in endless progression.

Labubu Clicker Core Mechanics

Labubu Clicker blends addictive tapping with Labubu's charm: earn coins per click, automate income via upgrades, and unlock new friends for fresh sessions.

Strategic progression lets you accumulate power-ups and auto-clickers for idle gains, while milestones reveal humorous antics and character variations.

Reach 100% on a Labubu to spawn blind boxes—tear them for new variants and build your unstoppable collection with Ascension Multipliers for boosted upgrades.

Simple controls: tap Labubu for coins, continuous clicks to open boxes, perfect for kids, fans, clicker pros, and casual players seeking relaxed fun.

Labubu Clicker delivers endless joy through its charming world—tap into the fun today and collect them all!
