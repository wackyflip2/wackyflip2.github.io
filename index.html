How to play Wacky Flip

Master the Controls

Wacky Flip’s controls are intuitive but demand precision:

  • Crouch: Click or tap and hold to build jump power. Hold longer for higher jumps.
  • Jump: Release to launch into the air.
  • Flip: Tap mid-air to initiate a flip or trick. Hold to complete the move.
  • Land: Time your release to align with the target platform for a perfect landing.

Mistime any step, and your character will tumble in hilarious ragdoll fashion!

Progress Through Levels

The game features 8 levels, each with 10 flip challenges. You’ll start at The Court, mastering basic jumps, before progressing to complex environments like The Site 2 or The Playground 2. Each level introduces new obstacles, such as moving platforms or shrinking landing zones, requiring sharper timing and skill.

Unlock New Tricks

  • Pike: A sleek, tucked flip with legs extended.
  • Scissor: A stylish mid-air leg split.
  • Knee Tuck: A fast, compact flip for tight landings.

Combine these tricks for massive combo multipliers and jaw-dropping scores.

Maximize Your Score

Scoring depends on trick complexity, landing accuracy, and combos. Chain multiple flips without crashing to boost your multiplier. In Arcade Mode, keep flipping until your energy depletes to chase high scores and climb global leaderboards.

Practice Makes Perfect

Use Practice Mode to hone your skills without penalties. Experiment with timing and tricks to nail challenging levels. Patience is key—later stages demand pinpoint accuracy to avoid obstacles like cranes or gaps.
